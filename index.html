Welcome to my personal webpage

I'm a PhD student at the Instituto Nacional de Astrofisica, Optica y Electronica (INAOE) located in Tonantzintla, Puebla, Mexico. My research interests include computer vision, deep learning, artificial intelligence and robotics. My current work focuses on deep learning for high-frequency camera pose estimation. I'm a member of the Intelligent UAS group at INAOE leaded by Prof. Jose Martinez-Carranza. Also, I have participated as a member of the QuetzalC++ team, winning first place in the Autonomous Drone Category at Robotics Mexican Tournament (TMR 2019).
